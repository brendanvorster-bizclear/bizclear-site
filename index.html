Privacy Policy

Last updated: 18 April 2026

Overview

App Fast ("we", "our", "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

Information We Collect

App Fast collects minimal information, all stored locally on your device:

  • App selections: Which apps you choose to manage and the tier you assign to each. This is stored on-device only.
  • Usage statistics: How many times you kept fasting, stayed strong, or broke the fast. These stats are generated and stored locally.
  • Encouraged app logs: Time you voluntarily log in positive apps. Stored locally.
  • Settings and preferences: Your tier defaults, affirmation text, and other configuration. Stored locally.

Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect, view, or transmit any content from your apps
  • We do not read your messages, emails, or browsing history
  • We do not track your location
  • We do not collect personal identification information
  • We do not use analytics or tracking SDKs
  • We do not share any data with third parties
  • We do not display advertisements

Screen Time API Usage

App Fast uses Apple's Screen Time API (FamilyControls, ManagedSettings, DeviceActivity) solely for the purpose of managing app shields on your device. The API operates within Apple's privacy framework, which means the app receives opaque tokens for selected apps rather than identifiable information about them. All shield management happens on-device.

Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device using iOS standard storage mechanisms (UserDefaults and the app's shared container). No data is transmitted to external servers. Uninstalling App Fast removes all locally stored data.

Children's Privacy

App Fast is not designed for or directed at children under 13. It is a personal device usage management tool for individuals managing their own screen time.

Common Questions

My selected apps aren't being shielded

App Fast needs Screen Time authorisation to place shields. If shielding stops working:

  • Open Settings → Screen Time and confirm Screen Time is on for your device
  • Open App Fast and make sure your apps are still selected under Settings
  • If nothing shows up, tap "Choose Apps" and re-select them — iOS occasionally clears selections after system updates
  • Restart your device and try again

I can't bypass the shield when I really need to

That's intentional — each tier has a different bypass experience:

  • Gut Check — a brief affirmation, then you can continue
  • Light Fast — a mindful intervention screen before you decide
  • Full Fast — no bypass, by design

If you need access to a Full Fast app, open App Fast and change that app to a lighter tier. Your choice, your pace.

How do I change which apps are shielded or their tier?

Open App Fast, go to Settings, and adjust your selection or per-app tier. Changes take effect immediately.

Do you collect my data?

No. App Fast runs entirely on your device. Nothing is sent to our servers — we don't have any. See our Privacy Policy for details.
